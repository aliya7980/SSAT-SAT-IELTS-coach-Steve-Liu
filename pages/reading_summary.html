Life is not only about exams, scores, and homework. This page collects the quieter and warmer parts of life: books I enjoy, time with cats and dogs, outdoor walks, hiking trails, family outings, music, and hobbies.

These moments remind me that learning is connected to real life. Reading builds imagination. Animals teach patience and loyalty. Nature gives us space to think. Hobbies keep curiosity alive.

New Cat Blog

I added a warm little cat-writing corner with short blog pieces about the bond between humans and cats, how cats show affection, and a photo essay featuring tuxedo cats, calicos, tabbies, and gingers.

The writing is gentle and personal, with research-backed ideas about attachment, companionship, slow blinking, purring, and the quiet rituals that make cats feel like family.
