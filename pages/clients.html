YuWei: Full-Mark SSAT Achievement

YuWei was a very talented yet diligent student from Shanghai American School. Through focused SSAT coaching, she learned to pair her natural ability with disciplined reading habits, precise vocabulary work, and steady test strategy.

Her progress reflected the core of my SSAT teaching: diagnose weaknesses clearly, build skills patiently, and train students to think with accuracy under time pressure. The result was an outstanding full-mark performance on the SSAT Middle Level.
