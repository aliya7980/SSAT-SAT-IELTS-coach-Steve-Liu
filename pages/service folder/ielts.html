Course Content

Listening Section

  • Practice with a variety of audio recordings (conversations, lectures, discussions)
  • Develop listening strategies for different question types
  • Improve note-taking skills and speed

Reading Section

  • Skimming, scanning, and detailed reading techniques
  • Time management strategies for handling long reading passages
  • Answering question types such as true/false/not given, matching headings, and multiple choice

Writing Section

Speaking Section

  • Strategies for answering all three parts of the speaking test
  • Pronunciation practice and improving lexical range
