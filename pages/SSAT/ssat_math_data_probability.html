SSAT Math: Data & Probability

Mean, median, range, tables, graphs, probability, and counting.

Core Knowledge

Mean: add values and divide by the number of values.
Median: the middle value after sorting.
Range: greatest value minus least value.
Probability: favorable outcomes ÷ total possible outcomes.

SSAT Strategy

Slow down when reading tables and graphs. Check labels, units, and whether a question asks for a number, fraction, percent, or comparison.

Practice Questions

  1. Find the mean of 8, 10, 10, 12, and 15.
  2. Find the median of 4, 9, 2, 12, 7.
  3. Find the range of 18, 23, 14, 31, 20.
  4. A bag has 5 red marbles, 3 blue marbles, and 2 green marbles. What is the probability of choosing a blue marble?
  5. A student can choose 1 of 4 shirts and 1 of 3 hats. How many outfit combinations are possible?
  6. In a class of 24 students, 6 chose soccer as their favorite sport. What percent chose soccer?
Show Answer Key

1. 11. The sum is 55, and 55 ÷ 5 = 11.

2. 7. Sort the list: 2, 4, 7, 9, 12.

3. 17. 31 - 14 = 17.

4. 3/10. There are 10 marbles total and 3 are blue.

5. 12. Multiply 4 shirt choices by 3 hat choices.

6. 25%. 6 ÷ 24 = 1/4 = 25%.
