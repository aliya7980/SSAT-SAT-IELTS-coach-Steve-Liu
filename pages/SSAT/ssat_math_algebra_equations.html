SSAT Math: Algebra & Equations

Variables, equations, inequalities, expressions, and coordinate patterns.

Core Knowledge

Combine like terms: 3x + 5x = 8x, but 3x + 5 is not 8x.
Balance equations: whatever you do to one side, do to the other.
Distribute: 4(x + 3) = 4x + 12.
Slope idea: rate of change = change in y ÷ change in x.

SSAT Strategy

If an equation looks messy, simplify both sides first. For answer-choice questions, substitution can be faster than full solving.

Practice Questions

  1. Solve: 3x + 7 = 25.
  2. Simplify: 4a + 3b - a + 2b.
  3. Solve: 5(y - 2) = 30.
  4. If 2x - 5 > 9, what must be true about x?
  5. A line passes through (2, 5) and (6, 13). What is its slope?
  6. If f(x) = 2x² - 1, what is f(3)?
Show Answer Key

1. x = 6. Subtract 7, then divide by 3.

2. 3a + 5b. Combine a terms and b terms separately.

3. y = 8. Divide by 5 to get y - 2 = 6.

4. x > 7. Add 5 to get 2x > 14, then divide by 2.

5. 2. Slope = (13 - 5) ÷ (6 - 2) = 8 ÷ 4.

6. 17. f(3) = 2(9) - 1 = 17.
