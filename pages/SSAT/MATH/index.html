Study Sequence

1. Arithmetic & Number Operations

Integers, factors, multiples, PEMDAS, exponents, roots, fractions, decimals, and estimation.

2. Fractions, Ratios & Percents

Proportions, percentages, percent change, discounts, and ratio reasoning.

3. Algebra

Expressions, equations, inequalities, slope, intercepts, and basic systems.

4. Word Problems

Distance, work, age, money, mixtures, percent change, profit, and discounts.

5. Geometry

Angles, triangles, quadrilaterals, circles, coordinate geometry, surface area, and volume.

6. Data & Probability

Mean, median, mode, range, tables, charts, graphs, and basic probability.

7. Logic & Quantitative Reasoning

Patterns, sequences, missing values, and logical deduction.

8. Mixed Timed Sets

Short mixed drills for accuracy, pacing, and test-day decision making.

High-Frequency SSAT Path

Arithmetic → Fractions → Ratios → Algebra → Word Problems → Geometry → Mixed Timed Sets

This sequence builds fluency first, then reasoning, then timing. It is designed to help stronger SSAT students move from roughly 60-70% accuracy toward 85-95% accuracy.
