Mixed Timed Sets

Short mixed drills for pacing, accuracy, and deciding when to skip or estimate.

Timing Plan

Set a 12-minute timer. Try the first pass quickly. Circle any question that feels slow, then come back after finishing the easier problems.

Mixed Set A

  1. Evaluate: 6² - 4(5).
  2. 15 is what percent of 60?
  3. Solve: 2x + 9 = 31.
  4. A rectangle has area 48 and width 6. What is its length?
  5. The mean of 4, 8, 10, and x is 9. Find x.
  6. A car travels 150 miles in 3 hours. What is its speed?
  7. Find the next number: 4, 8, 16, 32, ___.
  8. A circle has diameter 12. What is its radius?
Show Answer Key

1. 16.

2. 25%.

3. x = 11.

4. 8.

5. 14.

6. 50 mph.

7. 64.

8. 6.
