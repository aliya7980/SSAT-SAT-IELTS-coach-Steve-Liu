SSAT Math: Geometry & Measurement

Angles, triangles, circles, area, perimeter, volume, and coordinate geometry.

Core Knowledge

Triangle angles: the three interior angles add to 180°.
Area formulas: rectangle = lw, triangle = 1/2 bh, circle = πr².
Perimeter: add all outside lengths.
Volume: rectangular prism = length × width × height.

SSAT Strategy

Redraw messy figures. Mark equal sides, right angles, parallel lines, and missing angle relationships before calculating.

Practice Questions

  1. A triangle has angles of 45° and 65°. What is the third angle?
  2. A rectangle is 9 cm long and 4 cm wide. What is its area?
  3. A circle has radius 5. What is its circumference in terms of π?
  4. A rectangular prism measures 3 by 4 by 8. What is its volume?
  5. A square has perimeter 52. What is the length of one side?
  6. What is the distance between points (1, 2) and (1, 9)?
Show Answer Key

1. 70°. 180 - 45 - 65 = 70.

2. 36 cm². Area = 9 × 4.

3. 10π. Circumference = 2πr = 2π(5).

4. 96 cubic units. Volume = 3 × 4 × 8.

5. 13. A square has four equal sides, so 52 ÷ 4 = 13.

6. 7. The x-values are the same, so the vertical distance is 9 - 2.
