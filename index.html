Reading Comprehension

Read the following passage and answer the question below:

"The scientific method is a systematic approach to research that involves making observations, forming hypotheses, conducting experiments, and drawing conclusions. This method has been fundamental to scientific progress throughout history."

What is the main purpose of the scientific method?

Explanation:

The correct answer is C. The passage clearly states that the scientific method is a "systematic approach to research," which directly corresponds to option C. The other options contradict the information provided in the passage.
